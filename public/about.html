About HeyMeg

Built by a creator.
For every creator.

HeyMeg wasn't born in a boardroom. It was built from firsthand experience — the late nights, the scattered notes, the missed opportunities, and the relentless pressure to show up consistently online.

The Origin

It started with
a very real problem.

"I had the audience. I had the ideas. What I didn't have was a system that could hold all of it together."

Our founder built a community of over 2 million people across platforms — not by accident, but through years of showing up, creating, iterating, and figuring things out in real time.

But behind the scenes? The workflow was a mess. Brand deals tracked in email threads. Content ideas scattered across different apps. Goals written on sticky notes that eventually disappeared.

Every tool out there was built to be everything for everyone — flexible by design, but never quite right for this specific job. Nothing was built around the actual workflow of a creator: the content planning, the brand deals, the goals, the day-to-day. Just generic platforms asking you to bend yourself around them. So instead of settling, they built something new.

HeyMeg is the platform our founder wished had existed from the beginning.
